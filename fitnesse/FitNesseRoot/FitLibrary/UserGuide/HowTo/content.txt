!2 How to, in storytests
 * Use several do fixtures in flow in the same storytest. See SelectFixture
 * Edit nested tables:
  * EditingNestedTablesInFitNesse
 * Ignore some tables in a page/file:
  * ^IgnoreTables
 * Avoid lots of similar workflow storytests, which are boring, difficult to change, and rather verbose:
  * ^ExtractCalculations
